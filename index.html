About Me


I have received a bachelor’s degree in Economics from Stony Brook University.

I have also received both a Master’s Degree in Geography and a Geographic Information Science (GIS) certificate from Hunter College.

My research interests includes spatial patterns of urbanization, the impacts of urbanization, and environmental changes on populations. I am currently working with professor Deborah Balk and professor Bryan Jones.
